About Us

History

Pioneer Trading Corporation, founded by Shree Dhan Raj Mundhra, has been in the bearing business since 1960 and is currently managed by partner and son, Govind Mundhra.

Imports

We are the authorized importers of ZWZ, HCH and WZWN bearings. Along with that we are the owners of VPZ and SRK India brands.

Business

We are open to new ideas on business relationships. We endeavour to serve our associates with high quality reasonably priced bearings and can supply items which are not in our stock with the cooperation of our manufactures.
